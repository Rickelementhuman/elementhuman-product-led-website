Hi, HX Design.

Ok, so what is HX design?

Human Experience design (or HX design!) is a design framework which combines a number of existing design principles such as user research, UI design and design thinking with empathy at its core.

WHY WE USE IT

HX Design in a nutshell.

01

What matters is the experience.

In contrast to other disciplines, HX design doesn’t focus on designing for a specific type of person (for instance, a user, a client...) but for any human who could be connected to or influenced by the product, system or service at any point in the life cycle.

The idea behind HX design is to “think human” and take human perspectives, emotional states and emotional wellbeing into account.

02

How do we use it?

HX design is at the core of our business. We believe that humans should be taken into account at all times. To achieve this, we use HX design as a framework and apply it through HX methods.

A design sprint is a 4 day process to solve big challenges and create new products. It compresses months of work into a few days.

We use design sprints to enable our clients to transform their businesses into Human AI businesses. After an HX Design Sprint we move straight into a human-driven development cycle to bring our brand new ideas to life.

The HX community is a co-creation community made up of our beta clients. We believe our clients are the experts in their field and aim to connect our clients’ business decisions with our technology.
