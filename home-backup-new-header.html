What we do
HX Data
How it works
We measure and predict the ​human experience. The precision of a behavioral research lab, the scale of a survey, the speed of modern marketing.

We help creators, agencies, brands, and platforms turn human behaviors into machine learned empathy.
‍
Our technology tests content, using environmental context and machine learning to simulate, then model the real world human experience at scale. We call these behavioral learning models (BLMs).

This allows machines (and the humans operating them) to recognize, interpret, and empathize with the human element.
Across 11 years, 30.73 billion  collected across, 90 countries, and 226 brands, we've developed a machine learned solution to understand how drivers of human behavior inform experiences.

The combination of audience sourcing, survey, psychological testing, mock environments, attention tracking, biometric data, and emotional modelling is our secret sauce.
Freedom within a standard framework. Our methodology brings the rigor, our rich tapestry of data brings the creative freedom.

We test creative assets inside mock platform environments by capturing, encoding, then modelling human data.

Our data is comprised of biometrics, attention tracking, psychological tests, and survey responses from real humans - like you and me - through everyday devices like smartphones and laptops.

This data, collected in context, is encoded and modelled to simulate the real world performance of an asset.

Add in our notorious benchmarks and voila! Measuring your campaign's impact just became more science than art.
