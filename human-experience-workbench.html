Audiences

And/or bring your own audience using our custom source tooling.

Attention Model

Eye tracking, heatmaps, biometric time series curves
Measures attention pixel by pixel, frame by frame.  
Illuminates where and when brands capture attention.
Diagnose attention increases and lapses in the creative.

Memory Model

Psychological test (IAT) to measure subconscious memory and cognition
Reveals hidden drivers of consumer behavior.
Measures campaign effectiveness in the mind of consumers.
Tracks brand perception and brand memorability.

Emotion Model

Facial coding + deep learning + personalization = empathy
Facial Action Coding System categorization, tuned to the individual.
Identifies the 6 basic emotions, frame by frame.
Measures expression levels on a time series, independent of individual emotions.
